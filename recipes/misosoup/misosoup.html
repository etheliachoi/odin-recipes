Miso Soup

Description

A warm and hearty dish that is perfect for a quick breakfast! One of my bf's specialty dishes :D

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tsp dashi granules
  • 3 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 package silken tofu
  • 2 chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Add water and dashi granules into a saucepan and cook over medium-high heat. Bring it to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in the miso paste.
  3. Chop up the tofu and green onions and stir them in.
  4. Gently simmer for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
